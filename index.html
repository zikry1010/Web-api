Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer 30-day return policy for all products. Items must be in original condition with all accessories.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

Are the phones unlocked?

All our phones are factory unlocked and compatible with most carriers worldwide.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery is available for next-day arrival.
